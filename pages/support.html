Frequently Asked Questions

How do I join a tournament?
Players can browse available tournaments on the platform dashboard and register their team by paying the required entry fee if applicable.
How do I withdraw my winnings?
Players can request withdrawals through the wallet section. Withdrawals are processed according to the platform's withdrawal policies.
What happens if a player cheats?
WarriorZone enforces strict anti-cheat rules. Players found using cheats or unfair advantages may be banned from tournaments or suspended.
Can I create my own team?
Yes. Players can create teams, invite friends, and register their squad for tournaments directly through the platform.

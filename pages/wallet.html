Wallet & Payments

The WarriorZone wallet allows players to manage tournament entry fees, receive rewards, and track their transactions securely. All financial activities within the platform are handled through the wallet system.

Deposits

Players can add funds to their WarriorZone wallet using the available payment methods supported by the platform. Deposited funds can be used to pay tournament entry fees or participate in competitive matches.

Tournament Entry Fees

Some tournaments require a small entry fee. This fee contributes to the prize pool for the tournament and ensures competitive participation. Entry fees are automatically deducted from the player's wallet.

Prize Distribution

Winning teams receive rewards which are credited directly to their WarriorZone wallet once the tournament results are verified by administrators.

Withdrawals

Players can withdraw their wallet balance according to the platform's withdrawal policies. Withdrawals are processed after verification to ensure secure transactions.

Transaction History

Every wallet activity is recorded. Players can view their complete transaction history including deposits, tournament fees, rewards, and withdrawals.

Secure Transactions

WarriorZone uses secure systems to protect wallet transactions and ensure that financial activities are handled safely and transparently.

Payment Policies

Transparent System

All wallet transactions are clearly displayed in the user's account dashboard, allowing players to track their activity.

Fair Prize Distribution

Rewards are distributed based on tournament results after moderator verification to ensure fair outcomes.

Secure Withdrawals

Withdrawal requests are processed through secure payment channels to protect players and prevent fraudulent activities.

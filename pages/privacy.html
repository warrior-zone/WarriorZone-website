Privacy Policy

WarriorZone values the privacy of its users. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect personal information when users access and interact with the WarriorZone platform.

1. Information We Collect

WarriorZone may collect certain information when users register or interact with the platform. This information may include:

  • Name and profile information
  • Email address
  • Account login credentials
  • Tournament participation data
  • Wallet and transaction history
  • Device and browser information

2. How We Use Information

Collected information is used to operate and improve the WarriorZone platform. This includes:

  • Creating and managing user accounts
  • Processing tournament registrations
  • Managing wallet transactions
  • Providing customer support
  • Improving platform functionality
  • Preventing fraudulent or abusive activity

3. Data Security

WarriorZone implements appropriate security measures to protect user information against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

While we take reasonable steps to secure data, no online system can guarantee absolute security.

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

The platform may use cookies or similar technologies to enhance user experience, analyze site performance, and maintain login sessions.

Users may control cookie settings through their browser preferences.

5. Third-Party Services

WarriorZone may use trusted third-party services for payment processing, analytics, or platform infrastructure.

These services may process limited user data according to their own privacy policies.

6. User Rights

Users may request access to their personal information or request corrections if the data is inaccurate.

Users may also request account deletion according to the platform's policies and applicable regulations.

7. Data Retention

WarriorZone retains user information only as long as necessary to provide services, comply with legal obligations, and resolve disputes.

8. Changes to Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes in platform operations or legal requirements.

Users are encouraged to review the policy regularly for updates.

9. Contact Information

If users have questions about this Privacy Policy or their data, they may contact WarriorZone support through the platform's support page.

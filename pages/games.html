WarriorZone

Supported Games

WarriorZone hosts competitive tournaments for popular mobile esports games. Players can participate with their teams and compete against skilled opponents to prove their abilities and win rewards.

BGMI (Battlegrounds Mobile India)

BGMI is one of the most popular mobile battle royale games in India. WarriorZone organizes competitive BGMI tournaments where squads fight for survival on large maps while competing for the top leaderboard positions and tournament rewards.

Players must rely on teamwork, strategy, and precise gameplay to outlast other squads and secure victory.

Free Fire Max

Free Fire Max delivers fast-paced battle royale gameplay designed for mobile devices. WarriorZone tournaments for Free Fire Max allow players to compete in intense matches against other teams in a competitive esports environment.

Quick decision-making, team coordination, and survival skills play a key role in achieving success in Free Fire Max tournaments.

Tournament Format

Squad Based Matches

Most WarriorZone tournaments are played in squad formats where teams compete together to achieve the best placement and score points through eliminations and survival.

Leaderboard Scoring

Teams earn points based on match placement and eliminations. The leaderboard is updated after each match to determine the top-performing teams in the tournament.

Moderator Verified Results

All results are reviewed by moderators to ensure accuracy and fair competition before rewards are distributed.

Future Game Support

WarriorZone plans to expand its esports ecosystem by supporting additional competitive games in the future. Our goal is to build a platform where gamers from different titles can compete and grow their esports careers.

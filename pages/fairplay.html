Fair Play Policy

WarriorZone is committed to maintaining a fair and competitive esports environment. All players must follow the platform rules and respect the principles of honest gameplay and sportsmanship.

Anti-Cheat Policy

The use of cheats, hacks, exploits, or any unauthorized software that provides an unfair advantage is strictly prohibited. Players found using such tools may be immediately disqualified and permanently banned.

Account Integrity

Players must use their own accounts while participating in tournaments. Account sharing, impersonation, or using another player's account to compete is not allowed.

Match Manipulation

Any attempt to manipulate match outcomes, intentionally lose games, or collude with other players to influence tournament results is strictly forbidden.

Respectful Conduct

Players must maintain respectful behavior toward other participants, moderators, and the WarriorZone community. Harassment, abusive language, and toxic behavior are not tolerated.

Moderator Authority

Tournament moderators have the authority to review match results, investigate disputes, and enforce penalties when rules are violated. Their decisions are final in maintaining fair competition.

Penalty System

Violations of the fair play policy may result in warnings, tournament disqualification, temporary suspension, or permanent account bans depending on the severity of the offense.

Player Responsibilities

Follow Tournament Rules

Players must carefully read and follow the rules provided for each tournament to ensure smooth competition.

Report Violations

If players suspect cheating or unfair activity during a match, they should report it to moderators for investigation.

Maintain Competitive Integrity

Every participant is responsible for maintaining the integrity of the competition by playing honestly and respecting other players.
